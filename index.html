Where It All Began

I've always been fascinated by technology and the attention to detail its creators have. My journey as a developer began when my curiosity led me to discover coding bootcamps and their approach on teaching JavaScript. I was one of the lucky receipients of an Ironhack scholarship for expressing my passion of inclusion and the need for women in tech. Not long after, I hit the ground running with my first clone project and never looked back.

Full Stack Developer

During my time at Ironhack, I worked with an array of technologies to help me become a Full Stack Developer. I graduated mid pandemic in May 2020 with technical skills using JavaScript, MongoDB, Express.js, Node.js, React, HTML and CSS. Since graduation, I've worked on expanding my knowledge with each technology, and continue to strive for clean and efficient code. I enjoy working on projects with friends I've made in the dev community, challenging myself with codewars, and tackling bugs so I can see my code come to life.

Why Hire Me?

I am a dynamic and motivated professional with a personal mantra to 'never stop learning and improving'. With experience in building relationships and making well thought out decisions in high stress situations, my mission is to find an opportunity to be a part of an ambitious team where I can face new challenges and continue to develop the skills I have gained through Ironhack.
