Where It All Began

I've always been fascinated by technology and the attention to detail its creators have. My journey as a developer began when my curiosity led me to discover coding bootcamps and their approach on teaching JavaScript. I was one of the lucky receipients of an Ironhack scholarship for expressing my passion of inclusion and the need for women in tech. Not long after, I hit the ground running with my first clone project and never looked back.

Frontend Developer

My technical experience includes collaborating in an agile environment with multi-disciplinary teams of engineers and designers on a daily basis to deliver a greenfield project from inception to MVP. I have professional experience with Vue.js, JavaScript, TypeScript, Pinia, Jest, and Tailwind CSS. I also have experience with React.js, MongoDB, Node.js, and Express.js. I enjoy working on projects with friends I've made in the dev community and tackling bugs while striving for clean code.
